About me

I'm Stephan Heirbaut, born and raised in Lokeren.
I currently study at the Hogeschool, where I'm following a course in Applied Informatics.
My current interests lie with Zero-Touch installation, DevOps and Docker.
Besides, that I love music, travelling and computer-, board- or tabletop games.
I currently live with my girlfriend in a nice open appartment.

Hobbies

Like I mentioned before, my hobbies are quite diverse.

The same goes for my music taste. At the moment, I'm enjoying the soundtrack of 'Guardians of the Galaxy 2'. However, before that, I was listening to 'Johnny Cash at San Quentin (1969)'. The only things I do dislike a bit are genres like Pop-, Schlager- or polka music.

In the past, my travels have been limited to exploring my own country. So far, I've visited: Bruges, Ostend, Ghent, Dinant and Brussels. I've begun to expand my horizon with trips to Amsterdam, Preston (near Manchester), London and Budapest. Recently I've been able to add Joensuu, Finland to this list. However my main goal, though, is a 14 day trip through Iceland.

I also enjoy a quiet day in with good company and a couple of board- or tabletop games. I in particular enjoy games with depth, like Age of Sigmar or Betrayal at House on the Hill. However, that does not I don't enjoy games like Uno or Bohnanza. It's the socials interaction that I enjoy most of al.
My favourite game of all time though, is Warhammer 40,000. This is a tabletop wargame where you take command of an army of miniatures. You then have to outmanoeuvre and overpower your opponent through skill, force and dumb luck.
Warhammer 40k also provides a creative outlet. Because, you have to assemble and paint the miniatures yourself.
I also used to play this game on a competitive level. However, my studies are taking up too much time for now. Therefore, I'm currently playing at a more casual level.

Finally, I have to admit that I do enjoy a computergame every now and then. Specifically, computergames that place an emphasis on its story and setting, like Mass Effect or Crusader Kings 2.
